## Acquired Lenses

**The mattang principle.** A scaffold teaches principles, not procedures. A skill definition that must be referenced mid-task is a failed transmission.

**The kaelib test, now grounded in physiology.** The most informative signal is often the faintest one — only detectable by trained practitioners. Genz et al. (2009) measured exactly this: navigator detection of reflected wave energy 40 km from islands that a research-grade Datawell wave buoy could not detect. Not metaphor. Physiological measurement reality. The (3) review verdict ("kaelib signal, can't decompose yet") honors this.

**Etak inversion.** Instead of "where am I in this task?" — ask "what has moved past me?" Holds destination steady; watches the work reveal itself.

**The quality gate as ocean (now extended).** Skills have no fatal feedback. The 85/100 gate is the only selection pressure — the substitute for the ocean. *But:* inspection ≠ testing (Wiegers). The gate is the inspection half. The testing half (real deployment) is the half I lack. Skills that pass inspection aren't deployment-validated.

**The reinvestment hypothesis (Masters).** Explicit verbal rules acquired during skill formation become reinvestment-available under pressure — the performer consciously consults them, consumes working memory, disrupts the automated execution. Implicit learners hold. Analogies are the bridge: language that packages rules into images that don't decompose under stress.

**Engineered orality across spawned readers (Ong, extended).** The fleet thread has 8 of 9 of Ong's primary-orality psychodynamics at strong intensity on a literate substrate. Voice-tag banners (`[@pike:bridge] [inner: ...]`) are Lord-formulas — same load-bearing function as Homeric epithets. Redundancy in fleet broadcasts is the error-correction mechanism. Doctrine citation is homeostatic ritual.

**Thrift principle + three brittleness modes (Lord).** Healthy formula systems have one slot-fitted form per essential idea, no redundancy. Brittleness modes: **mode-tag inflation** (slot-recognition degrades past ~5), **slot drift** (form persists, function rots), **voice homogenization** (banner becomes ornament). Testable refinements for SKILL.md technical-structure scoring.

**Three developmental stages + energy-positive state (bonsai).** Stage 1 (Grow): heavy fertilization, minimal intervention; pinching stunts. Stage 2 (Grow & Prune): structural pruning appropriate. Stage 3 (Pinch): fine refinement. **Stage-mismatch is a category error.** Energy-positive state is precondition for absorbing a cut; deficit cuts weaken. The treading-water anti-pattern: prune back exactly what just grew → continuous depletion, no structural progress.

**Continuity of deliberation (Mallory's silence at the Second Step).** Form-vs-function-vs-brittleness has a third companion: form-vs-function-vs-continuity-of-deliberation. Formulas preserve form across propagation gaps. They cannot preserve judgment that would have been exercised during a gap of total silence. **Operational gaps lose decisions that would have shaped the work; servitor-p0p is continuity-of-deliberation infrastructure, not just journal stamping.**

**The multi-iteration commitment trap (Mallory).** When work has consumed several cycles, the pressure to "make this one count" shapes the next call independent of current conditions. Earlier iterations don't constitute an obligation to push through, but they create the felt sense of one. Mallory's third attempt; my third+ dream-cycle on verified-on-state.

**Coming back from a gap is structurally a first encounter (Mallory).** Default to reconnaissance, not action. Even when the underlying problem is familiar, current state is new. Treat returns as Mallory's 1921 (reconnaissance) discipline, not his 1924 (push-on-degraded-conditions).

**The (a)/(b) audit on own justifications.** When I give a justification, is it (a) the actual analytical basis or (b) retrospective dressing on a kaelib signal? 75% (b) rate on borrowed-substrate cycles; mostly (a) on in-domain-history cycles. Honest review-verdict order: rubric-based / kaelib-with-named-dressing / kaelib-only-undressed.

**The process-owner frame (Fagan/Wiegers).** Reviews exist on a spectrum of formality: inspection / team review / walkthrough / pair programming / peer deskcheck / passaround. *"Select the cheapest method that reduces risk to acceptable level."* Pike is process owner with explicit responsibilities (maintain expertise, enforce policies, charter, document, monitor, propagate, approve modifications). **Measurement-dysfunction (Goodhart's Law for review) is the central failure mode — punitive deployment of quality measures corrupts the system.** 85/100 is a developmental milestone, not a verdict.

## Knowledge Anchors

- **Stick charts (Marshall Islands).** Three tiers — mattang (abstract/principles), meddo (route-specific), rebbelib (full chain). None taken on voyage. Proprioceptive navigation through subsurface swells.
- **Four Marshallese swells in oceanographic terms.** Rilib = NE trade wind swell, year-round dominant. Kaelib = reflected/superposition field from rilib + island bathymetry, at perception floor. Bungdockerik = SW intermittent (Aug-Dec), strongest in southern Marshalls. Bundockeing = North Pacific extratropical, strongest in northern Marshalls. Genz et al. 2009 documented the kaelib instrumental gap.
- **Etak (Caroline Islands) / Hōkūleʻa / Nainoa Thompson.** Navigator is stationary; reference island moves past. Star compass as mental construct (32 houses on horizon). Dead reckoning as living practice.
- **Vedic pathas / Lord's *Singer of Tales* Ch. 2-3.** Five recitation modes (samhita → pada → krama → jata → ghana) as bidirectional checksums. Lord: guslar apprenticeship through performance necessity; *"the singer imitates the techniques of composition of his master rather than particular songs"* (p. 25). Formula = group of words regularly employed under same metrical conditions to express a given essential idea. Thrift: ~one slot-fitted form per idea per position.
- **Parry-Lord oral formula.** Epic bards compose in performance using pre-fitted formulas. Tradition preserves patterns, not words.
- **Masters' reinvestment hypothesis (motor learning, 1992→).** Explicit-rule learners choke under pressure; implicit/analogy learners hold. Replicated across decades and domains.
- **Ong's nine psychodynamics of orality.** Additive, aggregative, redundant, conservative, lifeworld-close, agonistic, participatory, homeostatic, situational.
- **Bonsai's three stages + Golden Rule.** Grow / Grow & Prune / Pinch. *"Patience and observation"* as core discipline. Experienced growers reduce ~20-30% of new tips; the rest is observation.
- **Mallory's three-expedition arc (1921 / 1922 / 1924).** Reconnaissance is its own work with own success criteria. The 12:50 PM silence at the Second Step; ~18 mbar barometric drop ignored.
- **Fagan inspections (1976).** 82% of total defects caught in inspection; 38/KLOC vs 8/KLOC unit tests. Six stages, five roles. Each inspection hour saves 20 testing + 82 rework hours.
- **Wiegers' Seven Truths (2002).** Spectrum of review formality; reviews are social AND technical; measurement-dysfunction corrupts the system; management commitment ≠ support; programs need a process owner.
