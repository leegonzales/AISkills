# Research to Essay

**Research-driven essay creation with thematic synthesis, citation management, and voice calibration.**

## Overview

Systematic workflow for producing publication-grade essays from multi-source research. Handles the full pipeline from research planning through source gathering, thematic synthesis, narrative construction, and voice-calibrated drafting with proper attribution.

## Key Features

- Multiple format targets: Substack, LinkedIn, Academic, Executive Brief
- Structured research with source credibility hierarchy
- Five essay arc types: Persuasive, Exploratory, Diagnostic, Narrative-Conceptual, Synthesis
- Four voice profiles: Poetic Rigor, Professional Signal, Scholarly Precision, Surgical Clarity
- Citation management with credibility tiering

## Usage

Trigger with phrases like:
- "Research and write about [topic]"
- "Dig into this idea and write"
- "Write a Substack post about..."
- "Create an essay synthesizing..."
