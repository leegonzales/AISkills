# Command Center Builder

Build a personalized AI command center as a Claude project in 15 minutes.

## What It Does

Guides you through configuring a Claude project that acts as your personal operations center — connected to your work tools, tuned to your communication style, and configured with the workflows you actually need.

## What You Get

1. **Project Instructions** — Custom instructions that define your command center's personality, behavior, and capabilities
2. **Voice Print** — Rules that make Claude write like you, not like a robot
3. **MCP Setup Guide** — Step-by-step for connecting your tools (Google Workspace, Slack, Jira, Confluence)
4. **Quick-Start Card** — One-page reference of commands to try first

## Installation

### Claude Web (claude.ai)
1. Open Claude → Settings → Skills
2. Upload the `.skill` file
3. Start a new conversation and say "Build my command center"

### Claude Code
1. Copy `command-center-builder/` to `.claude/skills/`
2. Invoke with `/command-center-builder`

## Setup Flow (10-15 minutes)

1. **Quick Profile** (2 min) — Name, role, priorities, tools
2. **Voice Print** (3 min) — Paste writing samples, get style rules
3. **Persona Selection** (2 min) — Choose your AI's personality (JARVIS, ALFRED, FRIDAY, Sidekick, Vanilla, or Custom)
4. **Workflow Config** (3 min) — Briefing style, urgency handling, draft preferences
5. **Generate & Deliver** (5 min) — All outputs ready to paste into your new project

## Persona Options

| Persona | Vibe | Best For |
|---------|------|----------|
| JARVIS | Proactive, anticipatory, slight wit | Leaders who want an AI that stays ahead of them |
| ALFRED | Measured, thorough, quietly brilliant | Those who value completeness and reasoning |
| FRIDAY | Efficient, data-forward, terse | Busy people who want signal, not personality |
| THE SIDEKICK | Energetic, informal, real talk | Those who want a sharp colleague, not a tool |
| VANILLA | Standard Claude + your voice | People who find personas distracting |
| CUSTOM | You describe it | Anyone with a specific vision |

## Requirements

- Claude account (personal, team, or enterprise)
- At least one MCP integration available (Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Slack, etc.)
- 15 minutes of your time
- 2-3 writing samples (Slack messages, emails — the more "you" they sound, the better)
